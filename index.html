About company

Energoprojekt Energodata was established in 1966 as part of Energoprojekt Group, as a company for development and application of information technology. Today it operates as a subsidiary part of the State of Energoprojekt. Energodata from that earlier period remembered and recognized as a regional pioneer of IT development, implementation and application of a variety of business software-hardware solutions based on its own technology and resources. The continuity of 50 years of successful operation, envious financial results, a recognized name in the Serbian IT world, rich partnerships, the most important references and educated young team led by experienced professionals Energodata today ranks among the most prominent ICT companies in the region.
